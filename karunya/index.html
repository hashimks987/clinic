Late Dr. Jacob John

Our Legacy

Established in 1985 by late Dr. Jacob John (ENT specialist), under his and Dr. Ammini Jacob's dedicated guidance, the hospital has prospered into a comprehensive healthcare institution.

We provide comprehensive care for all ENT-related problems, Head and Neck ailments, along with general medical conditions including Diabetes, Hypertension, and specialized Thyroid treatment clinics.

Leadership

The ENT department is currently led by Dr. Jerin Jacob, who brings exceptional expertise and modern medical practices to our institution. His credentials include:

  • MBBS from MOSC Medical College, Kolencherry
  • M.S. (ENT) from Yenepoya Medical College, Mangalore (2019 batch)
  • 2 years of experience as Assistant Professor at a reputed medical college
